Client type indicates whether the client application can keep its client secret confidential or not. The value is either "public" or "confidential". If your client application cannot keep its client secret confidential, choose "public". For example, choose "public" for Android/iOS applications.

The definition of client type is written in 2.1. Client Types of RFC 6749.

Note that "confidential" clients are always requested to present its client credentials at the token endpoint.
